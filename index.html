Fimi & Ta Sats

Fimi & Ta Sats:Its in collaboration with Velohouse contribute towards integration projects in Sweden. Our idea is to collaborate with new arrivals from different parts of the world into Swedish society by means of early-stage interventions and focusing on the individual. On Örestrand outside Höganäs we have done several projects for HVB home which leads to Personal Empowerment, Enhancing Personal & Professional knowledge and Networking including open house workshops, Swedish language learning and promoting Swedish handicrafts.

Management Team- David J. Hare, Jesper Rydberg.
